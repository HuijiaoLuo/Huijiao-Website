Burgers' equation

Burgers' equation is a non-linear hyperbolic equation occurring in various areas of applied mathematics. MacCormack scheme and Richtmyer/Lax–Wendroff method are the finite-difference methods (FDM) applied to this problem. In addition, Physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) provide another possible scheme for this problem.
